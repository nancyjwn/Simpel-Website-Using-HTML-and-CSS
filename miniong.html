About

The Apothecary Diaries (Kusuriya no Hitorigoto) is a historical mystery anime set in an ancient Chinese-inspired imperial court. The story follows Maomao, a bright and resourceful young woman with extensive knowledge of medicine and poisons. Initially working as a humble apothecary in the red-light district, she is kidnapped and sold into the imperial palace as a servant. Despite her initial reluctance, her exceptional skills soon attract the attention of Jinshi, a high-ranking eunuch, who recruits her to solve various medical and political mysteries within the court.

As Maomao navigates the palaceâ€™s intricate web of intrigue, she uncovers conspiracies, poisoning attempts, and hidden power struggles among concubines and officials. Her sharp intellect and unwavering curiosity lead her into dangerous yet fascinating situations, where she must balance her duty, personal safety, and the ever-present schemes of the imperial harem. With a blend of medical expertise, clever deduction, and dry wit, Maomao proves herself indispensable in the treacherous world of the palace, all while seeking her own freedom.
